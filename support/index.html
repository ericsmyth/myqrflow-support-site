FAQs

How do I scan a QR code?

Open the app and tap Scan QR Code. Point your camera at a QR code. When detected, follow the prompt to open a link or view content.

How do I generate a QR code?

Go to the Generate tab, choose a type (Website, Text, Contact, Wi‑Fi), fill in the fields, and tap Generate QR Code.

Where is my history stored?

On your device only. You can clear history from the History tab.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings → Upgrade and purchase the Pro upgrade to remove all advertisements.
