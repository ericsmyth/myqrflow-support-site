Privacy Policy

myQRflow is a simple QR code scanner and generator focused on speed and privacy.

Data We Collect

myQRflow does not collect or store personal data on our servers. Scanned and generated QR code history is stored locally on your device and can be cleared at any time from within the app.

Advertising

The free version may display ads provided by third‑party networks. These providers may use the device advertising identifier to deliver relevant ads. You can remove ads by upgrading to Pro inside the app.

Camera Access

The camera is used solely for scanning QR codes. No camera feeds are transmitted off your device.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email support@myqrflow.com.
